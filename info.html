Statistics of Substances

Affected at a Young Age

Those who have used an illegal drug before 15 y/o are 6.5 times more likely to develop a substance use disorder than those who wait until age 21 or later.

Life Expectency Drop

From 2019 to 2022, U.S. life expectancy declined annually due to opioid overdose deaths, with the reduction growing from 0.52 years in 2019 to 0.67 years in 2022.

Less Treatment

Among the 29.8 million individuals with Alcohol Use Disorder (AUD), less than 8% receive treatment.

Illegal College Drug Usage

97.2% of college students who use illegal drugs use marijuana, 1.1% use cocaine and 6.3% use hallucinogens.

Overdose Deaths

About 1.15 Million people in the U.S have died from overdose from substance since 1999
